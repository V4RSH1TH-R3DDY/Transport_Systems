Our Approach

TRAF-GNN employs a multi-view graph neural network architecture that captures spatial and temporal dependencies:

  • Physical Graph: Road network topology and connectivity
  • Proximity Graph: Geographic relationships between sensors
  • Correlation Graph: Traffic pattern similarities over time
